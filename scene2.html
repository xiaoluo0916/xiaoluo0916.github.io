Scene 2: Break down by crime type

Previoulsy, we saw that the incidents of crimes are significant, especially during 2015 and 2017, and after 2023. In the second scene, let's break down the crime count by primary type to understand what types of crimes were prevalent in Chicago.

x axis: crime type
y axis: number of crime count

Observations:

    - The top 10 prevalent crime types are: Theft, battery, criminal damage, deceptive practice, assault, other offense, narcotics, motor vehicle theft, burglary, robbery. These 10 crime types contribute to 92% of the overall crimes.
    - There have been over 200,000 incidents of theft, over 100,000 incidents of battery, and over 100,000 incidents of crimial damage since 2010, which is quite shocking.
