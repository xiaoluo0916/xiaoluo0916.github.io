Research on Crimes in Chicago

Scene 3: Break down by crime type, and whether the criminals were arrested

Previoulsy, we saw that the incidents of top 10 crime types are quite significant. In the third scene, let's further break down the crime count by primary type as well as whether the criminals were arrested to understand if the local authority was effective in arresting criminals.

Observations:

    - Within the top 10 prevalent crime types, narcotics has almost 100% arrest ratio, which is good to know. Battery, other offense and assault have around 20% arrest ratio. The other prevalent criminal types have less than 10% arrest ratio, which is not ideal.
    - Some crime types are more severe than the others in terms of the impact, such as narcotics, assault, criminal trespass, weapons violation, offense involving children, crime sexual assault, kidnapping, homicide, human trafficking. Only narcotics, criminal trespass, weapons violation have over 60% arrest ratio. Others have low arrest ratio, which is not ideal.
